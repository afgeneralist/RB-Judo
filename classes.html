Classes

Kid's Judo

The kid's class will focus on judo, a complete martial art composed of standing and wrestling skills. It's the preferred physical education program in many countries starting with Japan, especially for children.

Adults and older teens

Classes for adults and teens will consist of both judo and striking, which is Coach Jose's specialty. He is highly accomplished in both competition and rank on a national and international level.
