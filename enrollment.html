Enrollment

General Information

RB Martial arts accept students from approximately age four and up. New students may join at any time. We operate on a month to month basis. We do not have any contracts or registration fees. To get started all you need is to sign the registration form, be fitted into a uniform and pay your monthly training fee.

Fees are $75 for the month. However, families with multiple participants will pay $75 for the first, $50 for the second and $25 for the third.
