About us

RB Martial Arts is Judo America's newest judo training center located here in Rancho Bernardo. Jose Fernandez is the head coach of the club and will be teaching judo to kids and both striking and judo to adults. He has been affiliated with Judo America for ten years. Before coming to Judo America, he trained in both Karate and boxing for many years. In Karate, he is 3rd degree black belt where he has trained and competed for long periods of time in Japan. Furthermore, he was a top ranked amateur boxer in Canada having won many championships. Coach Jose has many years of experience coaching in martial arts and other sports. A father of four and a Rancho Bernardo resident for 19 years, you have probably seen him around the soccer field's with his family.

For questions or information please email coachjose@rbmartialarts.com or call 858 254-8162.
