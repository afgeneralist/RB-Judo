About us

RB Martial Arts is Judo America's newest training center located here in Rancho Bernardo. Coach Jose has been affiliated with Judo America for ten years. Before coming to Judo America, he trained in both Karate and boxing for many years. In Karate, he is 3rd degree black belt where he has trained and competed for long periods of time in Japan. Furthermore, he was a top ranked amateur boxer in Canada having won many championships. Stay tuned for his complete bio.
